Welcome to ***REMOVED***!

***REMOVED*** is a web application that provides novel insights about companies based on publicly available data. ***REMOVED*** uses the latest deep learning algorithms to understand a company's core operations, and provides several channels to explore the competitive landscape.

Beyond SIC codes

Commercial marketing, underwriting and due diligence provide estimates of a target company's value and risk by comparing it to its peers. Traditionally, peer identification has required SIC codes, discrete industry descriptions which are often overly-simplistic. ***REMOVED*** provides rich, quantifiable descriptions of a company's business extracted via deep learning algorithms, allowing you to move beyond SIC codes.

Features

Search

Find similar companies: give ***REMOVED*** a company name and it will identify the five next most similar companies.

Explore

Visualize segments: identify clusters of similar companies using ***REMOVED***'s interactive graphics.

Describe

Describe a company: describe the type of company you are looking for and ***REMOVED*** will find the closest matches.

Model

Model better: enhance model performance by adding ***REMOVED***'s dense vector embeddings to your feature set.
